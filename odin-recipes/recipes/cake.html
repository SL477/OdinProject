Cake

Description

This is a simple but good cake which you can customise as you like.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 4 ounces butter
  • 4 ounces I can't believe it's not butter
  • 8 ounces sugar
  • 8 ounces self-raising flour
  • For the buttercream icing: 4 ounces icing sugar & 2 ounces butter

Steps

  1. Mix the sugar & butter
  2. Beat in the eggs
  3. Fold in the flour
  4. Bake at 180Â°c for 20 minutes in greased baking tins. You may then need to leave it for a few more minutes. When it is cooked the sponge will bounce back when you press on it.
  5. For the buttercream icing boil some water. Then put the icing sugar and butter into a bowl. Drop a couple of drops of boiling water into the icing mix along with some melted chocolate. Mix the mixture
  6. After the cake has cooled put the icing in between the two halves of cake. Coat with melted chocolate and decorate as required.
